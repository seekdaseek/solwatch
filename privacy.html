SOLWATCH

PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

SolWatch collects only the information necessary to provide its services:

  • Solana wallet address (public key only) — used to record check-ins and mint badges
  • FCM device token — used to send push notifications for alerts and streak reminders
  • Check-in activity and streak data
  • Price and wallet alert preferences you configure

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect private keys, seed phrases, passwords, personal names, email addresses, or any financial information. We have no access to your wallet funds.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To record daily check-ins and maintain streak data
  • To mint cNFT badges and SBTs to your wallet
  • To send push notifications for price alerts, whale alerts, and streak milestones
  • To display leaderboard rankings

4. Data Storage

Check-in data is stored securely on Firebase Firestore. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

5. Blockchain Data

Check-in badges are minted as compressed NFTs on the Solana blockchain. Blockchain transactions are public and permanent by nature.

6. Third-Party Services

SolWatch uses Helius RPC for Solana network access and Firebase for data storage. These services have their own privacy policies.
